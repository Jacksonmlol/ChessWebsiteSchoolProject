About This Site

A school project built to help people learn chess

This site was created as a school project with a simple goal: help people get better at chess. Whether you've never touched a chess piece or you're looking to level up your game, the lessons here are handpicked to actually teach you something.

Chess is one of the oldest games in the world, but finding good learning resources can be overwhelming. There are thousands of hours of content out there — we cut through the noise and put the best beginner-to-advanced videos in one place.

All videos are embedded directly on the site so you can watch without leaving the page. Each one also has a link to open on YouTube if you prefer.

Topics covered include openings, tactics, middlegame strategy, endgames, and advanced play — with more to be added over time.

What's Included

  • Getting started & the rules of chess
  • Opening principles & popular systems
  • Tactics — forks, pins, skewers & more
  • Middlegame strategy & planning
  • Endgame technique & king & pawn endings
  • Advanced strategy from Grandmasters
